Mountain Biking

What is Mountain Biking?

Mountain biking is the activity of riding a bicycle on different kinds of off-road trails. It requires endurance, speed, balance and technical skills to navigate through rough terrains. Unlike road bikers, mountain bikers are don't affraid to face with obstacles and see the world as a natural and challenging playground full of winding trails. Vertical downhill sections, steep ascents, loose rocks, water crossings, tree roots: everything is an opportunity to test their abilities.

Biking is one of my favourite sports to keep healthy and to keep my weekends busy.It brings a solitude on the trails that causes us to forget about our day-to-day problems and responsibilities. Passing down hills and nature parks are my favourite in mountain biking.Before we started riding in the trials we have to make to improve balanc,Refine the wheel Lift,ride more,ride in the new places,take clinic.

Whatever you are a seasoned biker or the beginner,you need to find a perfect trial that match with your skill level or abbilities.For the people who are new to mountain biking,should go out with a guide is highly recommended.riding down trials in the wooded areas,one always must be careful their own safe way.In there we can meet with exciting and wild animals so we have prepared to protect ourselves.

Knowing how to handle a mountain bike, as well as need to feeling comfortable and confident with the equipments like bike saddle, the height of pedals, the breaks and the position of helmet.They are essential for this activity.Being able to fix any mechnical problems that may occure problem along the way is another necessary skill.

Mountain biking is a dynamic sport that involves a total body workout but if you are riding in street or road it will use only your leg strength.In riding a bike it not only using your legs ,heart and lungs strength but also using your upper body when you climbing with your bike or meneuvering with your arms.

Types of mountain biking

Types of mountain biking are Trial riding ,Cross-country cycling(or XC) ,Downhill biking ,All mountain and Enduro.Trial riding is a pleasureable riding on marked trials including uphills and downhills section.Cross-country cycling is the most popular discipline of mountain biking. It consists on completing a defined round circuit or point-to-point long trail, on a terrain that can range from a single track ―a narrow trail to fit one rider at a time―, to fire roads or even paved roads.

Cross-country cycling requires endurance and very good bike handling skills but it does not too much focus on speed.Downhill biking is a thrilling type of mountain biking, which focuses on descending a trail at a very high speed.All mountain combines cross-country cycling and downhill.It implies riding on a natural terrain full of obstacles, either up a mountain and downhill.

Enduro refers to a specific competition born in Europe ,which consists on different stages that a rider has to complete, including both uphills and downhills. It is the same as all mountain except for the fact that you are racing.

When you are travelling to a unknown destination, you should hire a guide for mountain biking.Hitting the off-road with a mountain guide is your best chance to have an enjoyable experience.A guide knows different trails on the area and can advise you on which one to choose depending on your experience, skills and wishes.

Feeling excited to hit the off-road? Make sure to check your equipment carefully, pick up a trail according to your level and stay hydrated throughout the way. Enjoy the ride!
