Outdoor Adventure

Hello Everyone! we are a group of teenagers who like to do outdoor sports and we would like to share about our sport experiences and knowledges. You can read our blogs and we hope you can know more about outdoor activities.

How we started?

The main reason which we made this group is all of our members have same purposes. We all enjoy doing outdoor activities like camping, mountain biking, sailing in the sea, enjoying the nature. All of our group members meet in EDUSN school and we did a project together about outdoor activities. Then we started interested in that and decided to founded this group in order to share about outdoor activities for the people who interested in this field.

Outdoor Activities

Outdoor recreation or outdoor activity refers to recreation engaged in out of doors, most commonly in natural settings. The activities that encompass outdoor recreation vary depending on the physical environment they are being carried out in. These activities can include fishing, hunting, backpacking, and horseback riding,riding a bike in natural places and can be completed individually or collectively.

Mountain biking

Mountain biking is the activity of riding a bicycle on different kinds of off-road trails. It requires endurance, speed, balance and technical skills to navigate through rough terrains. Unlike road bikers, mountain bikers are don't affraid to face with obstacles and see the world as a natural and challenging playground full of winding trails. Vertical downhill sections, steep ascents, loose rocks, water crossings, tree roots: everything is an opportunity to test their abilities....

camping in the forest

Camping is an outdoor activity involving overnight stays away from home, either without shelter or using basic shelter such as a tent or a recreational vehicle. Typically participants leave developed areas to spend time outdoors in more natural ones in pursuit of activities providing them enjoyment or an educational experience. The night (or more) spent outdoors distinguishes camping from day-tripping, picnicking and other similarly short-term recreational activities....
